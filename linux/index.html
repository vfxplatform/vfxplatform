Linux in VFX

After the early years of the industry were dominated by Silicon Graphics, Linux became the natural successor to SGIâ€™s IRIX. Over 20 years later, Linux is the primary operating system used on artist workstations at many studios. Two of the goals of the VFX Reference Platform specifically relate to Linux:

  • Reduce complexity and effort required to support Linux in VFX and Animation.
  • Lower the barrier to adopting Linux as a supported platform both for software providers and end-user studios.
This page contains information about activities and initiatives in support of these goals.
